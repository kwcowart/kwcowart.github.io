My Favorite Movies

Here are my top 3 favorite movies!

Alien (1979)

Image from Wikimedia Foundation and is covered under fair use because of it's low resolution and discussion of the movie in question.

Alien is a 1979 science fiction horror movie directed by Ridley Scott. The movie follows the crew of a starship who accidentally encounter a very dangerous alien creature that they are forced to survive against. In my opinion, this movie is one of the greatest horror movies of all time. First of all, I'm a sucker for science fiction, and the atmosphere and retro-futuristic aesthetics of the movie give it an otherworldly feeling that few movies have successfully instilled.

Inglourious Basterds

Image from Wikimedia Foundation and is covered under fair use because of it's low resolution and discussion of the movie in question.

Inglourious Basterds is a 2009 movie directed by Quentin Tarantino about two simultaneous (fictional) plots to assasinate Hitler during World War 2. I'm not a fan of most Tarantino movies, but for some reason I quite enjoy Inglourious Basterds. The movie does a great job with the comedic aspects while also taking the concept seriously.

District 9

Image from Wikimedia Foundation and is covered under fair use because of it's low resolution and discussion of the movie in question.

District 9 is a 2009 science fiction movie directed by Neill Blomkamp about an alien race that is confined to an internment camp in South Africa. The whole movie is a metaphor for apartheid and is inspired by the real life District 6. I think the movie does an excellent job of making its point while still being entertaining and engaging, although the film does have a number of shortcomings.
