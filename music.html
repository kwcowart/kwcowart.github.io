My Favorite Albums

Here are my top 3 favorite albums!

Twin Fantasy

Image from Wikimedia Foundation and is covered under fair use because of it's low resolution and discussion of the album in question.

Twin Fantasy by Car Seat Headrest is a 2011 indie rock album, later re-released in 2018. The emotion in each song on this album really resonated with me as a kid, and it was one the things that made me interested in music. To this day it remains my favorite album.

Wildflower

Image from Wikimedia Foundation and is covered under fair use because of it's low resolution and discussion of the album in question.

Wildflower is a 2016 plunderphoncis/hip hop album by The Avalanches. The songs on this album are hevily focused around samples and feature a mix between hip hop and psychadelic sound. I think it's really cool how an album made from a bunch of samples could sound so unique and incredible.

I'll Try Living Like This

Image from Last.FM and is covered under fair use because of it's low resolution and discussion of the album in question.

I'll Try Living Like This is a 2015 vaporwave album by death's dynamic shroud. I started getting into this album recently, but I've always been a fan of the vaporwave genre. That being said, I think this album stands out because many vaporwave songs don't really add too much beyond the base samples, but this album is great at that.
